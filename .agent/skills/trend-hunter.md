## Viral Frameworks (Veo 3.1 Optimized)

### 1. The "Product Reveal" (E-commerce)
* **Formula:** Extreme Close-up + Slow Motion + Physics Element (Water/Silk/Smoke).
* **Prompt Template:** "Commercial macro shot of a [Product Name]. [Physics Element] interacts with the product in slow motion. Professional studio lighting, 8k, photorealistic, sharp focus."

### 2. The "AI Dancer" (Motion Control)
* **Formula:** Human Motion + Style Transfer + Consistent Background.
* **Prompt Template:** "A professional dancer performing [Dance Style] in a [Environment]. The character is stylized as [Art Style] but retains realistic physics and cloth simulation. Smooth motion, cinematic framing."

### 3. The "Impossible Travel" (Creator)
* **Formula:** Seamless Transition between two contrasting worlds.
* **Prompt Template:** "POV shot walking through a [Location A] door that seamlessly opens into a [Location B]. Continuous camera movement, photorealistic transition, travel vlog style."

## Google Flow Tips
* Veo loves natural language. Don't list tags. Describe the motion.
* Always specify lighting (e.g., "Volumetric lighting", "Rembrandt lighting").
* Use terms like "Physics-based simulation" for liquids/cloth.
