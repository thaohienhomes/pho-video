## Google Veo Prompting Strategy
Unlike older models, Veo understands natural language and complex physics. Do not use "word salad" (tags). Use descriptive, flowing sentences.
* **Structure:** [Main Subject & Action] + [Camera Movement] + [Lighting & Atmosphere] + [Technical Specs].
* **Key Triggers for Veo:** "Photorealistic", "4k", "Smooth motion", "Physics-based", "Cinematic lighting".
